About Our Community

Building confidence through conversation and connection

The Mindful Readers is a non-profit organization dedicated to anyone who wants to improve their public speaking, communication skills, and become more fluent in English. We provide a judgment-free environment where people can practice speaking, share ideas, and learn to express themselves with confidence.

Our book discussions aren't just presentations—they're open conversations where anyone can respond, share different opinions, and contribute to meaningful dialogue. This is a discussion room where we practice public speaking, learn respectful communication, and grow together.

Founder's Voice

Our Mission

Creating a safe space where no one fears being judged for their voice or speaking style.

As someone who struggled with stammering throughout my childhood, I understand the pain of being laughed at for the way you speak. After years of practice, professional guidance, and countless hours of self-improvement, I realized that the solution wasn't just technical—it was having a supportive community to practice with.

There are countless individuals suffering in silence, afraid to speak up because of fear of judgment. That's why I created this club—a safe, supportive place with no age restrictions, no judgment, where you can share your thoughts, ideas, and beliefs without fear of becoming a joke.

We are here for you! Your voice matters, and together we'll help you find the confidence to share it.
